BigWhale.io &amp; its parent company is a privately-owned, Certik Audited, cryptocurrency financial intermediation firm based in Switzerland. Unlike traditional banks, we have been working with various under-banked or un-banked businesses &amp; individuals in specific industries since 2020 who are involved in highly profitable but capital-intensive activities such as THC / CBD Oil / Hemp production &amp; distribution based in South America and other countries, Russian &amp; other oil distributors / transporters, cryptocurrency mining firms &amp; exchanges, and similar high-growth, high-profit, capital-intensive under-banked or un-banked businesses.Traditional banks refuse to grant loans to such businesses, and these businesses are also often established in countries with restrictive laws or an unfriendly, highly bureaucratic business environment which is not conductive to their ability to raise capital from traditional sources unlike other general businesses.What is Financial Intermediation?Financial intermediation is the action of taking deposits and making loans. A business involved in financial intermediation, for example, a bank, takes deposits or funds from a pool of investors (account holders) paying them a certain interest on their deposits, and then makes loans to borrowers at a higher interest rate than the one the bank pays to its depositors.The difference between the interest paid to them by their borrowers, and the interest the bank pays to its depositors is the bank's main source of income and profit.How it all began - Four friends and one borrower.We started out in 2020 as a team of just four investors who pooled money together and started lending money out to these crypto businesses, who offered to pay us anywhere from 2.2-5% daily interest depending on their individual profiles. At first, we were skeptical about it - as these sort of interest rates are unheard of, so we dug in deeper with a lot of research before meeting and approving our very first borrower.The first borrower.â€‹â€‹We started out with just one borrower based in China - they had a toy manufacturing factory as their "front", and in the back they had a hidden large-scale setup with a bunch of top-of-the-line servers and hundreds of mining rigs for different crypto coins. Some of these servers ran operations such as complex trading, liquidity &amp; arbitrage operations on major crypto exchanges (in and outside of China), while other servers scanned all sorts of crypto currencies / coins and determined mining profitability &amp; efficiency and other factors for all of these different crypto currencies, which was then fed to the mining farm team who then mined these coins on hundreds of top-of-the-line mining rigs.While this was a highly profitable business for them, they had a major problem: China has very restrictive cryptocurrency laws â€” they not only had to keep their crypto operations hidden with a toy factory as a front, since crypto mining and related operations are banned in China; they also could not raise money for this business, even though it is extremely profitable, further expansion is super capital-intensive due to the sheer size of servers, tech, equipment, staff, land and other assets required &amp; obviously no bank in China would let them in the door, much less grant loans to them.The first profitable year.As 2020 came to a close, the four of us made a little over $4 million (USD) in profit with just two borrowers we worked with within our first year of operation.What started out as a casual discussion among four friends, turned into a highly profitable business within its first year of conception. The future for us looked even brighter, and it indeed was.We went on to on-board 50+ borrowers from across the world over the next three years. From 2020 when we first started, up to 2023, we worked with these borrowers all over the world and made several millions of dollars in profit from our financial intermediation business, except for a few bad clients here and there who had to be written off as bad debts.We were able to take loans ourselves from traditional banks based in our home country Switzerland, and then loan these funds out to our borrowers who paid us interest rates which are unheard of in any traditional business industry, except for the cryptocurrency industry.The decision to raise funds from the public, and the birth of BigWhale.ioAs we approached the beginning of 2023, the four of us sat down again to discuss a plan to scale up our operations and take it to the next level. With traditional banks in our home base of Switzerland, we were able to raise only so much money before we started running into line-of-credit limits, tax compliance issues and other problems which come along with dealing with traditional banks and fiat currency, even in Switzerland â€” which has one of the most modern and open banking laws in the world. A legal Hemp farm which supplies hemp for producers of Cannabidiol (CBD) Oil - a completely legal medicine in many countries with growing popularity. If you're interested, check out this Forbes article explaining some of the benefits of CBD oil. We strictly make loan advances to legal businesses which benefit and provide value to their end users / consumers.Additionally, due to the nature of some of the loans we provide (legal THC / CBD / Hemp producers / distributors, Russian oil distributors etc.), we had problems with the Swiss FTA (Federal Tax Authority) and other agencies due to arbitrary EU and other laws related to such products (we had to explain we only lend funds to businesses producing / distributing these products where it is legal within their jurisdiction / country) - this along with a lot of other problems which come with scaling up a unique operation like this, led us to look for an off-shore, non-fiat route, one which could also allow us to not only scale our operations globally and attract a lot more borrowers and investors (without one-on-one meetings and time-intensive tasks which limit scaling) - but scale the business to new heights without all of the trouble with tax authorities, banks and other agencies - DeFi was the solution to our scaling plans.The Birth of BigWhale.ioOne of us had the idea, what if we built a DeFi platform online, and we allowed borrowers from all over the world to submit loan applications, and borrow from us on a locked-in smart contract to prevent bad debts, and at the same time, we let depositors sign up on the platform, where they can deposit money into our smart contract, where we would pay them interest for providing us funds which would allow us to grant and fund these loans we would make to our borrowers.Over two months of planning and designing the whole concept, and another month of intense development of smart contracts on the Binance Smart Chain for both borrowers and our stakers / depositors, having multiple security audits for these contracts, and ultimately putting it all together in the form of an online DeFi dApp &amp; website, BigWhale.io was born and launched on 14th March 2023.We designed a DeFi BNB borrowing &amp; staking dApp, which pays our stakers (depositors / investors) daily interest rates ranging from 0.8% to 2% based on their deposit amounts, while our borrowers pay us anywhere from 2.2 to 5% daily interest based on their individual profiles and business health.The Road AheadBigWhale aims to be the first truly decentralized finance (DeFi) bank that intends to provide financial services to borrowers from different industries, especially those who have been turned away by traditional banks. BigWhale aims to be a DeFi bank that caters to investors of all kinds and offers them fair interest rates for the liquidity they provide.
